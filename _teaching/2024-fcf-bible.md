In 2008, I began teaching the Bible to students at Wright State University in formal and informal capacities. I led a discussion group with Chi Alpha Campus Ministries for around 2 years. I also led many studies during intermissions through topics such as "Creation Science" and "Apologetics" consisting of 7 to 20 students.

Around 2012, I began teaching studies at Faith Christian Fellowship church (FCF) exegetically through books of the Bible to a group of around 15 to 20 church members. Through this ministry, I've taught through Galatians, Ephesians, James, Daniel, the Parables of Jesus, 1-3 John, Early Church History, Ecclesiastes, Colossians, and Hosea. Glad to have the opportunity to teach the church twice a month for around 11+ years. In 2017, I taught a mini-series on apologetics at FCF

In summary, I have experience teaching on a technical level.
