YMCA / Zen-Ryu Martial Arts
------
**2006 - May 2015** (9 yrs 5 mos)
I taught martial arts as an assistant teacher from 2006-2009 in two large schools in Wilmington and Jamestown Ohio. There were around 30 students at each school and I taught kata and assisted in demonstrations.

Faith Christian Fellowship
------
After a short break, a started my own school in Beavercreek Ohio in 2013. I trained around ten students, some through blue belt. The program had a Christian emphasis and concluded with a five minute Bible lesson. The program ended when I left for my Wycliffe internship. After returning to Ohio, getting married and students who found other activities in my absence ended the program.
