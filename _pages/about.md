About Me
========

I am a multidisciplinary software engineer and researcher with a background that spans the full technology stack—from the bare metal of embedded systems to the predictive power of data science and the user-facing architecture of web development. Having built systems across these domains, I specialize in bridging the gap between hardware constraints, complex data pipelines, and scalable software solutions.

Currently, my focus is on high-performance computing and systems programming. I am deeply interested in leveraging **Rust** and **C++** to develop robust, memory-safe, and highly efficient architectures. I am actively applying these tools to the fields of **Simulation and Modeling**, where I can combine my background in hardware-level execution and data-driven algorithms to create high-fidelity digital representations of complex, real-world systems.

**Background & Expertise**

* **Embedded Systems:** Extensive experience working close to the metal. I understand the intricacies of hardware-software integration, real-time processing constraints, and optimizing code for environments with limited compute power and memory.
* **Data Science & Machine Learning:** A strong foundation in extracting actionable insights from complex datasets. I have experience designing algorithms, evaluating adversarial robustness, and building predictive models that can be deployed in human-in-the-loop applications.
* **Web Development:** Proficient in designing and deploying the APIs, databases, and interfaces required to make complex backend systems accessible, scalable, and intuitive for end-users.

Whether I am optimizing a low-level routine, training a model on sensor data, or building a high-performance simulation engine, my goal remains the same: writing clean, secure, and performant code that solves complex engineering problems.
